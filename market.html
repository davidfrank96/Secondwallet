Market Woman Scheme

Second Wallet is working to improve the lives of market women through the Market Woman Scheme, which aims to provide financial support through low interest loans. By helping these women achieve financial stability and success, Second Wallet is working to create a brighter future for them. Hear what those who have benefited has to say about it
